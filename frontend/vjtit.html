ABOUT TECHNOVANZA

Technovanza, one of Asia’s largest student organised Science, Techno-managerial college festival of VJTI, Mumbai. It was born in 2000, with an aim of "Taking Technology To the Society." Technovanza conducts 60+ different events for promoting different sectors of engineering, which has over 170 colleges participating from all over India. Due to inundating and thirst for creativity among our students and unparallel guidance from our faculty, has grown to become one of the most awaited Techno-management event of the country. Reaching out to students and professionals alike, Technovanza has grown exponentially in the past few years and boasts on over 30,000 footfall and it promises to be bigger this year.
