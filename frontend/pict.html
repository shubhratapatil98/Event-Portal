About IMPETUS AND CONCEPTS '17

Impetus and Concepts is a technical event organized by PICT. This technical fest has ten events namely-Concepts, Pradnya, Pratibha, Swayantra, Tantra, Yantra, Net Wars, Social-Hackathon Sustainability Quiz and Internship Fair which will be held on 24th, 25th and 26th of March 2017. INC is an intercollegiate national level competition which has been catching the attention of corporate giants for sponsorship as well as guidance to budding entrepreneurs. Every year INC sets a platform for students to design, exhibit and watch their ideas come true. About 2400+ odd students participated from 150+ engineering colleges throughout India last year and we're confident to see even more this year.

Pradnya

C/C++ Coding

If C/C++ is your forte then Pradnya is the competition for you. Pradnya tests how strong your C/C++ game is with tricky MCQs and goes on to survey your programming skills with a problem statement.

Pratibha

Ideas on Comprehensive Technical Research

Pratibha is a Paper Presentation event to portray your talent and in depth knowledge in technical areas of your interest.Aspire to fulfil the expectations of the judges in the form of PAPER PRESENTATION.

NetWars

Real Time Computer Networking Challenges

Net-wars is a real time network based competition which consists of 3 rounds: NCQ, Net-Hunt, Net-Disenar.



Tantra

Tantra-1:PIC18 micro­controller based event.Tantra-2:Digital Signal Processing (DSP) based problem solving competition
