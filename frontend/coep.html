About Mindspark

FacebookTwitter MindSpark - 2016, the technical festival of COEP Pune is going to be held from 23rd to 25th September. MindSpark believes on a popular saying by Nikola Tesla "The spread of civilization may be likened to a fire; first a feeble spark, next a flickering flame, then a mighty blaze, ever increasing in speed and power." With a footfall of around 20,000 every year; MindSpark has grown to become one of the best technical platforms for showcasing engineering brilliance and competing among the best. This glorious edition of MindSpark promises sustainable ideas which will leave a definite mark in the history of tech-evolution. COEP being one of the oldest engineering college of India, carries 162 years of glorious history. So an engineer must participate @TechnoSpark not only to showcase his/her technical talent, but also to explore the stories behind the historic buildings of the institute.
